## Objective

Seamless QR turns a picture into a quiet doorway. It is designed for invitations,
posters, personal artwork, and small-business promotions where a conventional
black-and-white code would interrupt the composition.

## How it works

The creator runs entirely in the browser. Safe, Balanced, and Stealth test Q and
H error correction across every QR mask. Ghost Lab expands that search to M/Q/H,
real geometric angles from -20 to +20 degrees, and scales from 22% to 88% while
ranking local texture, edge alignment, saliency, finder contrast, and required
tonal change. The adaptive renderer retains source color and only adds the
camera signal needed by the selected recipe. Every downloadable result must be
discovered in the full artwork, decode to the exact link in both jsQR and ZXing,
and meet the selected per-decoder stress-test threshold.

## Current state

The public beta supports anonymous static HTTP and HTTPS destinations, Safe,
Balanced, experimental Stealth, and screen-first Ghost Lab scan targets. Ghost
Lab requires exact pristine decoding in both engines plus seven of twelve stress
variants per decoder, and can return Most hidden, Best balance, and Most reliable
validated poses without rerunning. Pictures never leave the device. Accounts,
editable Event Links, billing, saved projects, analytics, cloud vision ranking,
and QR-conditioned GPU art remain deliberate follow-on work.
