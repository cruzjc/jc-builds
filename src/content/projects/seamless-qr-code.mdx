## Objective

Seamless QR turns a picture into a quiet doorway. It is designed for invitations,
posters, personal artwork, and small-business promotions where a conventional
black-and-white code would interrupt the composition.

## How it works

The creator runs entirely in the browser. It tests Q and H error correction
across every QR mask, finds a low-impact placement, preserves finder and timing
structure, and changes local luminance while retaining the artwork's color.
Every downloadable result must decode to the exact link in both jsQR and ZXing
and meet the selected stress-test threshold.

## Current state

The public beta supports anonymous static HTTP and HTTPS destinations, three
camouflage levels, automatic or manual placement, local validation, and PNG
export. Pictures never leave the device. Accounts, editable Event Links,
billing, saved projects, analytics, and GPU-generated QR art remain deliberate
follow-on work.
