## Objective

Opening-Momentum v1 turns early-session US-equity data into bounded trade decisions. Claude can nominate a short list of symbols, but Alpaca/IEX market and account data plus fixed risk rules independently determine whether a setup qualifies, calculate size and prices, and control the order lifecycle. The workflow is deployed on the Raspberry Pi with notification-only scheduling active and the autonomous timer disabled behind independent safety locks.

## Strategy boundaries

- New entries are long-only and require the configured opening-range, VWAP, catalyst, spread, and liquidity conditions.
- Incomplete or missing opening-range data is non-actionable; the engine does not infer a trade plan from partial inputs.
- Failed-gap and opening-range failure logic protects only long positions owned by this strategy; it does not open short positions or sell unrelated holdings.
- Alpaca/IEX is the decision-time source of truth for bars, quotes, account state, positions, and market context.
- Claude may nominate at most five same-session `long_candidate` symbols. Its unattended runner exposes only web search and fetch tools; local shell, file, browser, persistence, skill, and configured MCP surfaces are disabled. Price, quantity, side, stop, target, order type, timing, command, and unknown fields are rejected.
- A new entry requires a fresh observed breakout, executable ask within a deterministic limit, current catalyst and liquidity checks, PDT and live-equity policy, a one-entry-per-session cap, and a maximum proposed notional of $50.
- Entries use whole-share DAY limit brackets after the breakout is observed. Protective exits cancel related strategy orders, re-read the live remaining long quantity, and use a risk-reducing DAY market sell. Explicit Alpaca `position_intent`, stable per-session client IDs, durable broker evidence, and reconciliation prevent blind retry.

## Current state

Market jobs use DST-aware timers instead of fixed UTC or Hawaii offsets. The deployed autonomous-capable service supervises the session through a 10:30 ET flatten and a 10:35 ET explicit failure deadline, and can refresh the bounded Claude nomination while it runs. Live execution is nevertheless hard locked: the autonomous timer is disabled and inactive, environment authorization is false, and the external live-trading kill file is present. The notification-only timer remains enabled and active.

Verification passed 228 strategy tests and 77 dashboard tests on the Pi. Coverage includes malformed or oversized Claude signals, ambiguous broker responses, delayed bracket cancellation, partial fills, query-only timeout recovery, deterministic exit retries, and unresolved-deadline failure. Deployment and read-only broker checks placed no paper or live orders.

Live activation remains blocked until the Alpaca account has an exclusive single writer with no manual trading, credentials are rotated and SSH is key-only, paper trading validates Alpaca `position_intent` request and response behavior, and the owner gives explicit authorization.
