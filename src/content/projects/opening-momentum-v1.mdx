## Objective

Opening-Momentum v1 turns early-session US-equity data into bounded trade decisions. Claude can nominate a short list of symbols, but Alpaca/IEX market and account data plus fixed risk rules independently determine whether a setup qualifies, calculate size and prices, and control the order lifecycle. The Raspberry Pi deployment now includes the renamed Trade Operations dashboard on its HTTPS `/trade/` route, while all live action remains behind independent safety locks.

## Strategy boundaries

- New entries are long-only and require the configured opening-range, VWAP, catalyst, spread, and liquidity conditions.
- Incomplete or missing opening-range data is non-actionable; the engine does not infer a trade plan from partial inputs.
- Failed-gap and opening-range failure logic protects only long positions owned by this strategy; it does not open short positions or sell unrelated holdings.
- Alpaca/IEX is the decision-time source of truth for bars, quotes, account state, positions, and market context.
- Claude may nominate at most five same-session `long_candidate` symbols. Its unattended runner exposes only web search and fetch tools; local shell, file, browser, persistence, skill, and configured MCP surfaces are disabled. Price, quantity, side, stop, target, order type, timing, command, and unknown fields are rejected.
- A new entry requires a fresh observed breakout, executable ask within a deterministic limit, current catalyst and liquidity checks, PDT and live-equity policy, a one-entry-per-session cap, and a maximum proposed notional of $50.
- Entries use whole-share DAY limit brackets after the breakout is observed. Protective exits cancel related strategy orders, re-read the live remaining long quantity, and use a risk-reducing DAY market sell. Explicit Alpaca `position_intent`, stable per-session client IDs, durable broker evidence, and reconciliation prevent blind retry.
- Trade Operations separates strategy-owned and external positions, recent automated-order activity, and recent manual-close activity. Its durable full-close workflow retains mutually exclusive strategy and external-position modes so an operator cannot apply both paths to the same action.

## Current state

The Trade Operations migration preserved the existing production records. Verification on the deployed Pi passed 308 strategy-source tests and 118 dashboard tests. The post-deployment broker snapshot contained one pre-existing externally owned position, no strategy-owned positions, and no open or same-day orders. No order was submitted during the release.

Live execution remains hard locked. The autonomous-entry timer and operator-exit timer are disabled, both live-action environment authorizations are `0`, and the kill file is present. Direct external close, strategy close, manual cancel, and live-entry controls are disabled. The durable close implementations remain deployed but cannot be invoked in this configuration.

Operational status is `ready_but_blocked`. Activation requires an exclusive single-writer Alpaca account, completion of the credential-rotation and key-only SSH prerequisites, paper validation of Alpaca `position_intent` request and response behavior, and explicit owner authorization.
