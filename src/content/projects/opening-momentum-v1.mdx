## Objective

Opening-Momentum v1 turns early-session US-equity data into explicit, reviewable trade plans. The strategy engine is deterministic: Alpaca/IEX market and account data plus fixed risk rules determine whether a setup qualifies. The monitored Raspberry Pi workflow routes qualifying opportunities to notification-only alerts for human review; it does not autonomously place orders.

## Strategy boundaries

- New entries are long-only and require the configured opening-range, VWAP, catalyst, spread, and liquidity conditions.
- Incomplete or missing opening-range data is non-actionable; the engine does not infer a trade plan from partial inputs.
- Failed-gap and opening-range failure logic protects held long positions; it does not open short positions, and an existing short position cannot generate a protective sell.
- Alpaca/IEX is the decision-time source of truth for bars, quotes, account state, positions, and market context.
- Claude may contribute research context only. Its unattended runner exposes only web search and fetch tools; local shell, file, browser, persistence, skill, and configured MCP surfaces are disabled. Deterministic rules decide whether an opportunity is actionable, and Claude is not an order-execution path.

## Current state

Market jobs use DST-aware timers instead of fixed UTC or Hawaii offsets. Qualifying alerts appear in a read-only dashboard section with no Execute control, and nearly expired entry alerts are suppressed. The current verification baseline is 31 strategy tests plus 13 app/context tests, with an HTTP 200 read-only smoke check and HTTP 401 checks for unauthenticated state and alert access. No test alert or order was sent, and the smoke check does not claim delivery to a phone or other end device.
