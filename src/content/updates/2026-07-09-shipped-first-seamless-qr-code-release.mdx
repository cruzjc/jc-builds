The first Seamless QR release is live as a public browser-based creator. Users
can upload PNG, JPEG, or WebP artwork, add a direct web destination, choose Safe,
Balanced, or Stealth camouflage, and position the QR region inside the image.

Generation stays on the user's device. A Web Worker tries multiple QR masks and
error-correction levels, protects structural scan landmarks, and validates the
exact destination with jsQR and ZXing across twelve camera-like stress cases.
Download remains locked until the selected confidence threshold passes.

This release intentionally ships the reliable static-link foundation first. It
does not yet include accounts, cloud image storage, editable Event Links,
billing, analytics, or GPU-generated art.
