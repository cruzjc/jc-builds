Published the first public Where Get Gas? pilot as a phone-first Sites app for
the main Hawaiian Islands.

## What shipped

- one-tap browser geolocation with no continuous tracking or IP fallback
- live HERE station prices, Matrix road miles and ETA, and an on-demand map
- Best Value, Lowest Price, and Closest rankings with transparent assumptions
- Regular, midgrade, premium, and diesel selection plus gallons and MPG presets
- explicit price freshness, provider attribution, and Apple or Google
  directions handoff
- public Privacy and Terms pages with no accounts, analytics, or saved searches

## Launch verification

The fixed central-Honolulu check returned 15 distinct Regular stations, all 15
with exact USD-per-gallon prices reported within 24 hours and all 15 with
usable driving routes. The public homepage, Privacy, Terms, live search,
short-lived map token, PNG map response, attribution, and no-store headers were
verified after deployment.

The release also passed 30 unit tests, eight rendered and integration checks,
type checking, linting, a production build, dependency audit, and credential
scans. Provider prices can still change, so the site never presents a displayed
price as a guarantee.
